### DEC-12 — Widget Session Token Architecture
source: docs/superpowers/specs/2026-05-19-widget-session-tokens-design.md
status: shipped (PR #29)
decision: JWT HS256 signed with APP_KEY. Claims: iss, sub (SHA-256(api_key+APP_KEY)), aud (canonical origin), ip, iat, exp. TTL 30 minutes. In-memory only (no localStorage). 7-day dual-accept migration window (WIDGET_SESSION_DUAL_ACCEPT=true default).
scope: SessionTokenService, RequireWidgetSessionToken middleware, widget.js, /api/v1/widget/init
