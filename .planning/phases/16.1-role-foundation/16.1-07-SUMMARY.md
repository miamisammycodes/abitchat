**2. [Rule 1 - Bug] DatabaseSeederMatrixTest: SQLite migrate:rollback incompatibility**
- **Found during:** Task 1 GREEN (teardown error after first test)
- **Issue:** `DatabaseMigrations` trait's `migrate:rollback` in teardown fails on SQLite for `make_users_tenant_id_nullable` migration. In-memory `:memory:` DB makes rollback unnecessary anyway.
- **Fix:** Override `runDatabaseMigrations()` to only call `migrate:fresh` + `db:seed`, skipping rollback
- **Files modified:** `tests/Unit/Database/DatabaseSeederMatrixTest.php`
- **Commit:** `be730db`

**3. [Rule 1 - Bug] PHPUnit 13 requires #[DataProvider] attribute not @dataProvider**

- **Files modified:** `tests/Feature/Auth/RoleMatrixTest.php`, `tests/Unit/Auth/RolePermissionsTest.php`
- **Commit:** `9884487`
