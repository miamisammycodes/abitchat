<objective>
Land the authorization decision layer: register every Ability as a Gate in `AppServiceProvider::boot()`, upgrade the 4 existing Eloquent policies to stack tenant-ownership + role-tier via `RolePermissions::can()`, and FIX THE TRIGGER — migrate `UpdateWebsiteIndexingRequest::authorize()` from the now-deleted `isOwner()` helper to `Gate::allows(Ability::ManageTenantSettings->value)`.

Purpose: This is the plan that makes the new authorization system actually take effect. Without Gates registered, `$this->authorize(...)` calls silently return true. Without stacked policies, a tenant Agent could delete Owner-tier resources (cross-tenant + within-tenant escalation). Without the UpdateWebsiteIndexingRequest fix, the 403 that triggered the whole phase still 500s.

Output: 1 modified provider, 4 modified policies, 1 modified FormRequest, 5 implemented tests.
</objective>
